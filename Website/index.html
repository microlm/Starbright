About Us

Hello, we are Lunar Rabbit Games, a small independent game company based out of Philadelphia, PA. We produce game in conjunction with the Drexel University Entrepreneurial Game Studio.

Games
Starbright

The stars collapse, the black holes grow, and the galaxy devours itself. From all this, a little star seeks to escape.

Manipulate the gravity of other planetary bodies to propel the star through space. Along the way, collect the smaller stars and planets to accrue enough mass to proceed to the next level. But hurry. The black holes continue to devour. Go too slowly and you may find yourself swallowed.
